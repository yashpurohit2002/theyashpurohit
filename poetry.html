Poetry

"My why?"

Poetry may just be the first thing I've done for myself
Seeking no validation but just my own truth
Seeking no aspiration, just a collection for my shelf
As I continue to live and write throughout my life's youth

This isn't me chasing a degree or some materialist gain
Nor is this a shot at fame in a crude and complicated way
This is just some writing when I have a thing to explain
Before I once again fall as the corporate world's prey

So please enjoy my journal about desires
A deep and vulnerable look into my life
Some are harsh, some maybe a tad dryer
And some writings are larger-than life

But at the end of the day, this is how I journal
How I jot my thoughts, emotions, and worries
Maybe this is just some way to make me eternal
Just before life catches up and hurries
